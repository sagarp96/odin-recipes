Pulao

This is Pulao. An indian breakfast dish, mostly prefereed with curd.

It's a rice dish made with basmati rice, spices and vegetables. It is a very popular breakfast dish in India. It is very easy to make and very delicious to eat

Ingrediants

  • Basmati rice
  • Vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, etc.)
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Green chilies
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, garam masala, etc.)
  • Salt
  • Oil or ghee
  • Water
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Rinse the basmati rice thoroughly and soak it in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and tomatoes, and cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add the vegetables and spices, and cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add the soaked rice and water, bring to a boil, then cover and simmer until the rice is cooked.
