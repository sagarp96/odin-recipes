Alooparatha

This is aloo paratha. An indian breakfast dish, mostly prefereed with curd.

It is made from wheat flour and mashed potatoes. It is a very popular breakfast dish in India. It is very easy to make and very delicious to eat

Ingrediants

  • Wheatflour
  • Potatoes
  • Spices
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Take wheat flour and mashed potatoes in a bowl and mix them well
  2. Make small balls from the mixture
  3. Roll the balls into a flat round shape
  4. Heat a pan and cook the flat round shape till it turns golden brown
