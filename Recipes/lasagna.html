Gulabjamun

This is Gulab jamun. An indian sweet dish.

It is made from milk solids and sugar. It is a very popular sweet dish in India. It is very easy to make and very delicious to eat

Ingrediants

  • milk solid
  • Sugar
  • Groundflour
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Take milk solid and groundflour in a bowl and mix them well
  2. Make small balls from the mixture
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry the balls till they turn golden brown
